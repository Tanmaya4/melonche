Founded in 1957, Meloche Windows & Doors is a family-owned manufacturer and installer based in Windsor-Essex. We manufacture our own windows and install them with our professional team, ensuring lifetime quality, value, and excellence.

Every project starts with a free, no-obligation in-home assessment. We custom-design and professionally install windows and doors tailored to fit your home's unique style and energy needs.
