FAQ

Answers to common questions about our window warranties, Energy Star ratings, and installation process.

questions
1

What products does Meloche provide?

Meloche Windows & Doors custom designs, manufactures, installs, and services vinyl and wood windows, and entry doors (steel, fiberglass, patio, garden, and storm doors) for new construction and replacement projects in Windsor-Essex.

2

Does Meloche provide a warranty?

Yes. We provide a lifetime warranty on both our premium window and door products and our professional installations. We stand behind our homegrown quality and team.

3

How does the assessment process work?

Every project starts with a free, no-obligation in-home assessment. One of our specialists will visit your home, measure your openings, discuss styles, and provide an accurate quote.

4

Are Meloche windows Energy Star Rated?

Yes. All Meloche windows are Energy Star Rated and meet or exceed local climate zone standards, ensuring maximum heating and cooling cost savings for your home.

5

Do you manufacture your own products?

Yes. We are one of the few companies in Essex County that controls the entire process: we design, manufacture, sell, install, and service all our window and door products from our facility in LaSalle, Ontario.

6

Do you work with home contractors?

Yes. Meloche is proud to partner with local Windsor-Essex contractors. We offer preferred contractor pricing, financing terms, and have over 65 years of industry experience.

7

What sizes of projects can you support?

We support projects of all sizes, from single window or front door replacements in family homes to full window packages for new construction, commercial buildings, and property developments.
